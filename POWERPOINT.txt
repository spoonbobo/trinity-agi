SLIDE 4 — HOW IT WORKS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

One Brain. Every Channel. Any Interface.

1. You talk — via text or voice
2. The agent thinks — powered by OpenClaw, a full AI engine with tools, memory, and governance
3. It acts — executes code, fetches data, calls APIs, runs workflows
4. It builds — pushes interactive UI surfaces to your screen in real time
5. You approve — dangerous actions require your explicit consent

Works from the web shell, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, mobile — same brain everywhere.
